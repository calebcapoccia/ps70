My plan was to then pause the print mid-print, add sand, then finish the print, such that the sand is forever enclosed in the hourglass.</p> <p>I originally planned to have the center diameter of the hour glass as 5mm diameter and the "glass" walls 1mm thick, but quickly realized this would lead to a relatively weak structure that might crumble, so I scaled up to a 3mm thick wall.
